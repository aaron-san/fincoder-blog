## Portfolio Management

### Bond Portfolio Management

#### Process

1. Set objective (return objective with benchmark (liabiity, bond index, etc.))
2. Develop strategy (passive, active, structured (mixed))
3. Monitor performance (measure and evaluate performance)
4. Make adjustments (consider costs of adjustment)

### Fixed-Income Portfolio Risks

- Interest rate risk
  - Parallel shifts
  - Measured by portfolio duration
- Spread risk
  - Changes in spreads between Treasuries and non-Treasuries
  - Measured by spread duration
- Yield curve risk
  - Exposure to twist in treasury yield curve
  - Measured by key rate durations, PV distribution of cash flow
- Credit risk
  - Exposure to downgrades and defaults
  - Measured by contribution to duration by credit rating
- Optionality risk
  - Exposure to change in cash flows due to call/put features
  - Measured by portfolio delta
